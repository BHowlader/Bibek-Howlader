BCI EEG Data Analysis

In this innovative project, I designed and developed an advanced Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) system powered by artificial intelligence to analyze Electroencephalography (EEG) signals and accurately classify various cognitive states. This breakthrough has far-reaching potential, opening doors to advancements in neurological research, assistive technology, and cognitive enhancement. My work demonstrates a seamless integration of AI and neuroscience, paving the way for transformative applications in understanding and enhancing human cognition.
