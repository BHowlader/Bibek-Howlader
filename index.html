HMPV Drug Prediction

This project focuses on predicting interactions between human and human metapneumovirus (HMPV) proteins using machine learning (ML) techniques, aiding in the discovery of potential drug targets. Understanding these interactions is crucial for identifying key viral-host protein relationships that could be targeted for therapeutic intervention.
BCI EEG Data Analysis

In this innovative project, We designed and developed an advanced Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) system powered by artificial intelligence to analyze Electroencephalography (EEG) signals and accurately classify various cognitive states. By harnessing cutting-edge deep learning techniques, this system decodes complex brain activity with an impressive accuracy of 98.81%. This breakthrough has far-reaching potential, opening doors to advancements in neurological research, assistive technology, and cognitive enhancement. My work demonstrates a seamless integration of AI and neuroscience, paving the way for transformative applications in understanding and enhancing human cognition.
